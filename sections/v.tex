\entry{Vertex}{
	A position (usually in 3D space) along with other information such 
	as color, normal vector and texture coordinates.
}

\entry{Volume}{
	Volumes are three-dimensional Actors \ref{Actor} used to alter 
	the behavior 
	of areas within levels. Volumes can be used for 
	behaviors like:

	\begin{itemize}
		\item Causing damage to the player or other Actors inside the Volume.
		\item Blocking certain Actors from entering the Volume, acting as a collision surface.
		\item Opening a door when an Actor enters the Volume.
		\item Changing the way a level calculates its lighting or visibility.
	\end{itemize}
}
